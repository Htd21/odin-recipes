White Bread Recipe For Bread Machine

Home Page

Easy to make white bread using a bread machine. Homemade bread are healthy and simple to make.

Ingredients

    245g warm milk
    45g white sugar
    4.5g salt
    50g vegetable oil
    408g bread flour; can also subsitute with all purpose flour
    10g active dry yeast

Steps

    Step 1 Add liquid first. Then add the dry ingredients into machine.
    Step 2 Select white bread setting. When done, let it cool before cutting into.
