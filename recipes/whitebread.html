White Bread Recipe For Bread Machine

Easy to make white bread using a bread machine. Homemade bread are healthy and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 245g warm milk
  • 45g white sugar
  • 4.5g salt
  • 50g vegetable oil
  • 408g bread flour; can also subsitute with all purpose flour
  • 10g active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Add liquid first. Then add the dry ingredients into machine.
  2. Select white bread setting. When done, let it cool before cutting into.
