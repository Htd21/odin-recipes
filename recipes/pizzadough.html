Basic Pizza Dough Recipe For Bread Machine

Easy to make pizza dough using a bread machine. Homemade pizza dough are healthy and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 245g Warm water
  • 50g white sugar
  • 5g salt
  • 50g olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 400g bread flour; can also subsitute with all purpose flour
  • 15g active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Add liquid first. Then add the dry ingredients into machine.
  2. Select the knead setting. When done, shape dough into ball. Dough ball can be freeze and use for later.
