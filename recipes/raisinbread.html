Raisin Bread Recipe For Bread Machine

Easy to make raisin bread using a bread machine. Homemade bread are healthy and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 245g warm milk
  • 30g white sugar
  • 4.5g salt
  • 40g vegetable oil
  • 375g bread flour; can also subsitute with all purpose flour
  • 10g active dry yeast
  • 2g cinnamon powder
  • 100g Raisins

Steps

  1. Add liquid first. Then add the dry ingredients into machine.
  2. Add raisins on the last mix cycle. On some bread machine there is a tray that you can put the raisins in and it will mix it in automatically.
  3. Select white bread setting. When done, let it cool before cutting into.
